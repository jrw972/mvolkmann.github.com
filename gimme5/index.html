Katy Trail Gimme 5

  • 5 mile out and back race entirely on the Katy Trail
  • Starts at the Family Arena in St. Charles and heads southwest
  • No entry fee!
  • No need to pre-register! Just show up 15 minutes before race time to sign the waiver.
  • Prizes
    Fleet Feet gift certificates to the first place finishers in these groups:
    overall male - $15
    male under 40 - $5
    male 40 and above - $5
    overall female - $15
    female under 40 - $5
    female 40 and above - $5
  • For more information call 636-928-3081.
